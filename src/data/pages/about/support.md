# Support

If you are interested in supporting this project or the creation of a similar project, please reach out via
email to [Elaine Yip](mailto:elaine.yip@ualberta.ca).

Funds would be used for:

- The purchase of flowers for on-site offerings. One stem of Chrysanthemum flowers is placed per site and
  purchased from local florist 藝濃軒 [(Arts and Florist)](http://ArtsandFlorist.com),
  a respectful gesture of greeting at each headstone visited prior to touching or brushing off dirt from the
  headstones.
- Digital transcription and analysis paid per hour of work.
- Data processing to update the website paid per hour of work.
